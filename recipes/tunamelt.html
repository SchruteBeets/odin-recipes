Tuna Melt

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 0.5 cup of canned tuna, strained
  • 1.5 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp of butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan on stovetop on medium-high heat.
  2. Assemble tuna melt by layering tuna and cheese.
  3. Add butter to preheated pan and melt evenly.
  4. Grill melt for 6 minutes total. 3 minutes on each side.
