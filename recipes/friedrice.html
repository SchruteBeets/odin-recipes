Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of cooked rice
  • 0.5 lb of pork tenderloins
  • 0.25 cup of vegetable oil
  • 0.5 cups of diced white onions
  • 1 cup of frozen vegetables
  • 3 large eggs,scrambled

Instructions

  1. Add small amount of oil to preheated wok.
  2. Cook pork for 8 minutes. Remove from pan.
  3. Add small amount of oil to wok.
  4. Add rice and cook for 5 minutes until rice is dry.
  5. Add frozen vegetables and white onions. Cook until translucent.
  6. Add scrambled eggs.
